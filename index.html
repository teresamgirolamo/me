About Me

    I am an incoming T32 postdoctoral trainee in the Cognitive Neuroscience of Communication at the University of Connecticut, where I will work with Dr. Inge-Marie Eigsti. I received my Ph.D. in Child Language at the University of Kansas, where I worked with Dr. Mabel L. Rice and was a T32 predoctoral trainee in Training Researchers in Language Impairment.

My Career/Research Interests

    Broadly, my interests lie in advancing equity & inclusion in society. To pursue this goal, I:
  • Work with autistic young adults in research to understand their experiences through language, with the broader aim of developing supports to meet their self-identified needs through community-based participatory research
  • Work on assessment methods and assessment development to ensure we meaningfully serve individuals from all walks of life through high-quality, culturally sensitive measurement, with the broader aim of understanding language acquisition across communities and within the context of human development
  • Work to ensure everyone has equitable access to opportunities and success in higher education. Currently I serve on the CAPCSD DEI Committee and am a member of the U21 Autism Research Network. I also developed comprehensive programming at KU with fellow minority students to advance equity & inclusion in CSD and higher education.

Research Training

    My doctoral training involved the use of behavioral techniques to investigate language acquisition and other life outcomes. In addition to training on and training others on the Language Acquisition Studies Lab protocols, this included data collection, scoring, and coding, as well as data processing, reliability, and analysis. My training also included the use of culturally sensitive, community-based methods to engage individuals from groups who are underrepresented in clinical research.

Miscellaneous

    My other interests include distance running, scheduling my life around my geriatric rescue pups' internal clocks, language and minority policy in Asia, travel, and cooking.
